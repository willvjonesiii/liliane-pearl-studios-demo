Meet the Founder

Shaping Musicians
from the Inside Out.

"Every child deserves a teacher who sees their potential before they see it themselves."

Liliane Pearl is a passionate music educator dedicated to developing the next generation of musicians. With years of performance and teaching experience, she founded Liliane Pearl Studios as a space where students of all backgrounds can discover the transformative power of music.

Her teaching philosophy centers on building strong fundamentals while keeping the joy of music alive — because when students love what they're learning, they become unstoppable.

Private Instruction

Programs We Offer

01

Piano

From beginner scales to advanced repertoire, our piano program builds technique and musicality at every level. Students explore classical, jazz, and popular styles.

02

Voice

Vocal training that develops breath control, range, tone, and stage confidence. Students find their authentic voice while exploring the music they love.

03

Violin

A rigorous yet joyful introduction to strings. Students learn proper posture, bowing technique, and musical expression from the very first lesson.

04

Music Theory

The language behind the music. Students develop their ear, learn to read notation, and gain the literacy that makes every great musician unstoppable.

Studio Tuition & Policies

From Tuition to Attendance,
Here's How We Partner with You.

From tuition to attendance, here's how we partner with students & families for success.

Tuition

Annual Coverage

Tuition covers a total of 35 classes throughout the year which includes weekly lessons, four group classes, 2 recitals, 2 makeup classes and additional examination prep (optional).

Our studio rate for half hour classes is $55*.

The studio offers 2 different payment options and bills at the following intervals:**

Studio Closure Policy

Closure Schedule

Lessons take place weekly, on a fixed day & time for 30, 45 or 60 minutes according to student level. The studio is closed for the month of August, 6 days at Thanksgiving (Tuesday – Sunday), 3 weeks at Christmas/New Years, 1 week for Spring Break, and most federal holidays. Each teacher has 3 additional sick/personal days which may be used at any point during the year with no affect to the schedule.

No refund is given during holiday closures as these weeks have already been withheld in the tuition calculation. For unexpected studio closures, credits will be given and applied to the next payment. Refunds will be given to those paying annually.

The Studio will be closed on the following Holidays:

  • Labor Day
  • Indigenous People's Day
  • Christmas and Chanukah
  • New Year's Day
  • MLK
  • Memorial Day
  • Juneteenth

Please note that there will be class on Veterans Day, November 11th (Tuesday).

Visit our Studio Calendar page or download our FULL Studio Calendar for a printable copy.

Summer Class Policy

Summer Schedule

Included in tuition are 5 private lessons and 1 group class between June and July. Students who continue lessons throughout the summer have significantly better outcomes than those who take a 3 month break.

We recognize that many families travel during the months of June–July. With this in mind, lessons will be pre-scheduled to accommodate. If you know in advance that you will not be able to attend at least half of your summer classes, please speak to our administrator before May 1st.

Please note that if you opt out of summer classes, your time slot will not be reserved for the fall.

Cancellation Policy

Cancellations & Makeups

Each student may take 2 makeup classes per year during a fixed "flex week." Please visit our Studio Calendar for these dates. All other cancellations are not rescheduled or refunded.

Please note: there are NO makeup lessons in the summer as classes are pre-scheduled. Teacher cancellations that exceed 3 days will be credited to your next payment or refunded.

Fees & Payment Options

Fees & Payment

There is a late payment fee of $15, which is assessed to any unpaid bills after the 1st calendar day of each semester.

Examination fees are based on grade and range anywhere from $100–$150 per exam.

The studio accepts payment via ACH bank transfer (No fees), and credit and debit card payments (3% fee). You may conveniently set this up during registration by following the prompts.

Please note the studio does not receive any surcharge — it covers processing fees.
