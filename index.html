Meet the Founder

Shaping Musicians
from the Inside Out.

"Every child deserves a teacher who sees their potential before they see it themselves."

Liliane Pearl is a passionate music educator dedicated to developing the next generation of musicians. With years of performance and teaching experience, she founded Liliane Pearl Studios as a space where students of all backgrounds can discover the transformative power of music.

Her teaching philosophy centers on building strong fundamentals while keeping the joy of music alive — because when students love what they're learning, they become unstoppable.

Private Instruction

Programs We Offer

01

Piano

From beginner scales to advanced repertoire, our piano program builds technique and musicality at every level. Students explore classical, jazz, and popular styles.

02

Voice

Vocal training that develops breath control, range, tone, and stage confidence. Students find their authentic voice while exploring the music they love.

03

Violin

A rigorous yet joyful introduction to strings. Students learn proper posture, bowing technique, and musical expression from the very first lesson.

04

Music Theory

The language behind the music. Students develop their ear, learn to read notation, and gain the literacy that makes every great musician literate in their craft.
